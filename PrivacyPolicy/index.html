Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

At Xenon Warp, we prioritize your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information. This application requires you to provide your own private server, as we do not offer any server infrastructure. Your data remains entirely in your control.

2. Data Collection

We may collect the following types of information:

  • 1.1. We Do Not Collect Sensitive Information: We do not gather, access, or store any of the following sensitive information:
    - Names (including usernames, screen names, or subscriber names)
    - Addresses (residential or business)
    - Telephone numbers
    - Payment details (credit card numbers, bank account information, or billing records)

    Additionally, we do not log or collect any data related to your online activities, such as:
    - Browsing history
    - Traffic destination
    - Data content
    - DNS queries
  • 1.2. Minimal Data Transmission: This application requires a private server provided by you. We do not offer or manage any servers on your behalf. When you use Xenon Warp to connect to a WireGuard-compatible server, the following information is transmitted by you voluntarily:
    - Your IP address
    - IP addresses of the sites you are connecting to
  • 1.3. App Permissions: Xenon Warp does not require or request access to sensitive permissions on your device, such as:
    - Contacts
    - Location
    - Device ID
    - Storage
    - Microphone
    - Camera

3. Data Usage

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data for us to use. Your privacy is fully respected at all times.

4. Data Sharing and Transfer

We do not share, transfer, or disclose any data to third parties. Your information remains entirely private.

5. Security Measures

We have implemented robust security measures to ensure the protection of any data transmitted through the application. Access to any system-related information is strictly limited to authorized personnel only.

6. Data Retention

We do not collect or store any data, so data retention is not applicable to our application.

7. Right to Be Forgotten

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, the "right to be forgotten" does not apply in the context of our application.

8. Third-Party Services

Xenon Warp is designed to work with your own privately deployed server. However, if you choose to use third-party services (e.g., Warp or Psiphon) through our application, we recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies to understand how they handle your data.
